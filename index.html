PRAMUKH GROUP

Corporate Outdoor Branding — Award-Winning Concept

The Brief

33 years in real estate. Rising competition. A legacy that needed to stand apart, without shouting.

The Insight

Class isn't spoken. It's built.

The Idea

An outdoor installation that behaves like the brand it represents: quiet by day, striking by night. Classic motifs: a sculpture, vintage binoculars, aged books, etc. rendered entirely in golden line art against black. By day: the full imagery reveals itself. By night: only the golden lines remain: a signature, not a shout.

THE LINE

"Class Isn't Spoken. It's Built."

The Result

  • Best Traditional Outdoor Award, 2026
  • Afaqs, Marketers' Excellence Awards, 2026

Credits

SOLEX ENERGY

360° Brand & Content Strategy

The Brief

A growing name in commercial, industrial, and utility-scale solar, needing a voice across every channel it showed up in, from LinkedIn to hoardings.

The Approach

One brand voice, built to flex; strategic on B2B platforms, sharp on social, human on video.

The Work

Strategy & IPs

Introduced recurring content formats: Monday Money Check, Solar Market Pulse, Before You Go Solar, turning one-off posts into an ongoing conversation.

Social & Performance

Meta ad copy across awareness, lead-generation, and retargeting funnels. Social media plans across LinkedIn and Instagram, balancing thought leadership with brand personality.

Film & Voice

Scripted and voiced YouTube brand films, including a company film built around workforce diversity and a 25-step quality process, made for a global audience.

Watch Video ↗

On Ground

B2B hoardings and print ads translating the same brand voice into physical, high-visibility spaces; for audiences who'd never see a Meta ad.

The Result

  • 17.1M views on Facebook — up 58%, with content interactions climbing 150%
  • 9.4M views on Instagram — up 96.6%, nearly doubling quarter-on-quarter
  • 4.8M impressions on LinkedIn — up 239%
  • Watch time on Facebook grew to 75 days, 10 hours — up 61.4%
